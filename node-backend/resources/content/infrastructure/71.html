Scaling

Perhaps one of the most powerful feature of Kubernetes is the ability for it to easily scale, this is where the desired state comes in. The desired state is having say for example 4 replicas.

With just a simple command:

kubectl scale deployments/your-app --replicas=<number>

notice below how the AVAILABLE pods started to increase, the ips have been generate in nodes, however only one ip is exposed acting as a load balancer.

λ kubectl scale deployments/sysoutprint --replicas=3
deployment "sysoutprint" scaled

deployment "sysoutprint" scaled
